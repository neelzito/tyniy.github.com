Description of Service

Tyniy is a photo and video compression application that allows users to:

  • Compress JPEG and HEIC images, as well as MP4, MOV, and other video formats with professional quality algorithms
  • Import photos and videos from external drives, SD cards, and media libraries
  • Resize photos and adjust video resolution to optimize file sizes and storage requirements
  • Export compressed photos and videos to various destinations including Photos app and Files
  • Batch processing for efficient workflow management

All processing is performed locally on your device with no data transmission to external servers.
