## Description (4000 characters max)
**Current: 2,580 characters**

Tired of running out of storage? Tyniy is the essential tool for photographers, videographers, and content creators who want to dramatically reduce photo and video file sizes without sacrificing quality. Whether your media is on your device, an external drive, or fresh off your camera, Tyniy shrinks your images and videos, reclaims gigabytes of storage, and speeds up your entire workflow.

**Key Features:**
- **Advanced Photo & Video Compression**: Our custom, hardware-accelerated algorithms intelligently reduce file sizes by 50-90% while preserving the details, colors, and textures that matter.
- **Support for Major Formats**: Compress JPEG, HEIC, MP4, and MOV files seamlessly.
- **Flexible Import & Skip Toggles**: Drag and drop whole folders and choose to skip photos or videos dynamically.
- **Customizable Video Settings**: Retain original video resolution or select optimized compression presets to fit your sharing and storage needs.
- **Powerful Batch Processing**: Compress your entire photoshoot or video collection at once. Tyniy handles dozens of files with ease.
- **Smart Downscaling**: For huge images (>25MP), Tyniy can automatically resize them to a web-and-share-friendly 18MP, unlocking even more storage.
- **EXIF & Metadata Preservation**: Your metadata is safe. Tyniy keeps original EXIF and video metadata, including camera settings, location, and timestamps.
- **Duplicate Prevention**: Tyniy automatically detects and skips already-compressed files, preventing re-compression.
- **Flexible Export Options**: Save your compressed media back to your Photos library, the Files app, or share them directly to any app on your device.

**Perfect for:**
- Professional photographers and videographers managing large media libraries
- Content creators optimizing images and videos for web and social media
- Anyone running low on device or iCloud storage
- Creative professionals who demand professional-grade results

**Why Choose Tyniy?**
- **Buy Once, Own Forever**: No subscriptions. No recurring fees.
- **100% Private**: All processing happens locally on your device. Your files are never uploaded to a server.
- **Professional Quality**: Maintains stunning visual quality while dramatically reducing file sizes.
- **Blazing Fast**: Hardware-accelerated on-device compression delivers quick results.
- **Simple & Efficient**: A clean, premium dark-themed interface designed for a fast workflow.

Download Tyniy now and transform how you manage your photos and videos!
